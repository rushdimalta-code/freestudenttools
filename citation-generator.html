Why Use a Free Citation Generator?

Incorrect citations are one of the most common reasons student essays lose marks. Our free citation generator formats references in APA 7th, MLA 9th, and Chicago 17th edition — the three most widely required citation styles at universities worldwide — without requiring an account or internet connection after the page loads.

The generator runs 100% in JavaScript. Enter the source details (book, journal, website, or film), choose your style, and get a perfectly formatted citation you can copy directly into your reference list. No data is sent anywhere.

Students use this for essays, dissertations, lab reports, and research papers. It supports books, journal articles, websites, newspaper articles, films, and podcast episodes across all three major citation formats.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the citation generator really free?

Yes — 100% free with no account, no sign-up, and no paid tier required. The tool runs entirely in your browser.

Which citation formats does it support?

The generator supports APA 7th edition, MLA 9th edition, and Chicago 17th edition (bibliography style).

Can I save multiple citations at once?

Yes. Use the Add to List button to collect citations, then use Copy All or Download as .txt to export the full list.

Does the tool auto-fill citations from a URL?

No — the tool does not fetch or scrape URLs. You enter the source details manually, which ensures accuracy. Always verify the final citation against your institution's style guide.
