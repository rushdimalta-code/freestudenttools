🇳🇱 Hague Academy of International Law

Hague Academy of International Law Scholarship

Open Now 🟡 Partial Funding MasterPhD High competition
Funding Package
Monthly grant of €800 + registration fee waiver for 3-week intensive sessions on public or private international law
Deadline: 1 December 2026  ·  Results: 31 March 2027

About This Scholarship

Graduate students and legal professionals from developing countries studying international law. Strong background in law required.

Hague Academy is the world's leading institution for international law. Short intensive programs (3 weeks) — certificates and diplomas, not degrees. Summer and winter sessions.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Graduate student or legal professional
  • Citizen of a developing country (priority for scholarship)
  • Study of international law
  • Apply through the Academy with CV and academic transcripts
  • French or English proficiency
