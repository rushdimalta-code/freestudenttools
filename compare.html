How to Compare Universities Effectively

Choosing between two universities involves more than rankings. Our free university comparison tool lets you compare any two of 1,040+ universities side-by-side across tuition fees, cost of living by city (190 cities), banking setup, accommodation costs, entry requirements, and course structures — all in one table.

The comparison pulls city-specific cost of living data (not just country averages), so you see the real monthly cost of student life in London vs Edinburgh, Toronto vs Vancouver, or Sydney vs Melbourne. Banking guides cover 28 countries, including local student bank accounts, international transfer services, and digital payment apps used by students in each country.

Use the comparison tool when narrowing down your application list, deciding between offer letters, calculating total study costs, or helping family understand the financial commitment of studying abroad.
